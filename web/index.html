How Job Finder works

        
A short tour of every feature so you can get the most out of the app.

      


      

        

          

            work
            
What is Job Finder?

          

          
Job Finder is a local AI-powered job search assistant. It reads your CV, scans LinkedIn and Indeed, scores listings against your profile, and helps you write targeted cover letters and prepare interviews.

        


        

          

            flag
            
Getting started

          

          
    
            
  1. Upload your CV in the Profile tab — Job Finder extracts your skills, experience and target roles.
    2. 
            
  2. Configure at least one AI provider key in Settings (Groq is free and easy to start).
    3. 
            
  3. Open Job Search, pick filters and keywords, then launch the scan.
    4. 
            
  4. Review results in the Dashboard and chat with the AI Coach about specific jobs.
    5.

Scanning & filters

          

          
In Job Search you can pick experience level (internship → senior), contract type (full-time, part-time, internship, contract, temporary) and work mode (remote, hybrid, on-site). The scan shows live progress with percentage and estimated time remaining.

        


        

          

            smart_toy
            
Chat with the AI Coach

          

          
    
            
  • Multiple chats: use the dropdown next to the coach to switch between separate conversations, or start a new one.
    • 
            
  • Pin jobs to a chat: open a job's details and click "Pin to chat" to give the coach the full description, then ask questions like "compare these two".
    • 
            
  • Cover letter: from a job's detail panel you can generate a cover letter targeted at the recruiter when their info is visible on LinkedIn.
    • 
          

        


        

          

            lock
            
Privacy

          

          
All your data (CV, jobs, chats, keys) is stored locally in a SQLite database under your user folder. Nothing is uploaded except your prompts to the AI provider you choose.
